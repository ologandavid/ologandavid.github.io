6.1151 : MicroComputer Project Laboratory (Spring 2022)

Designed a Modified Version of Duck Hunt Video Game using Two PSOC's. The smaller PSOC Stick operated the wireless controller while the larger PSOC handled game mechanics and updating the screen through VGA.

The controller sent 3 encoded bytes over Bluetooth to the larger PSOC which would decode it and update the cursor and target positions accordingly.

2.009: Project Engineering Processes (Fall 2021)

Designed and manufactured a compact aisle wheelchair with removable, height adjustable seat panels, an extendable back rest and seat, and deployable stabilization leg Rated for a 99th percentile male (350 pounds) with a 2x Safety Factor Prevents the uncomfortable lifting of paraplegic flyers from their wheelchairs to their airplane seat

Linear Rails and Carriages enable the sliding action Seat panels are lifted and raised with a winch situated in the back of the seat With a 10:1 gear ratio and non-back drivable winch, the lifting mechanism is safe and requires at most 10 pounds of force
